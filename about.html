About me

My name is Alan and I'm a self taught 13yr old artist from California. My interest in sculpting clay figurines started with playing with play doh. I progressed to sculpting with polymer clay, when I was about 8yrs old. Now I sell my clay figures at artwalks and festivals. My favorite subjects to sculpt are video game characters (like Mario) and animals. I also enjoy other things like playing on my Wii U and programming on Scratch. I hope you enjoy my creations and please make sure you are following me on Instagram.
